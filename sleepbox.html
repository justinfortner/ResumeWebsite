About Sleep Box

The Sleep Box aims to improve the sleep quilty of their users by reducing the dependancy on the clock portion of an alarm clock. A large number of people are stressed throughout the night by waking up, checking their phones to see how much time they have left to sleep. The sleep bo aims to let the user know that they still have enough time to fall back asleep without the use of numbers. thus eliminating that source of stress during the night. The sleep box works as follows. The user connects to the Sleep Box via bluetooth and to set the alarm to the desired time. The light will then change to a calming blue, suitable for illumination throughout the night. For the 30 minuites before the alarm is meant to sound the light changes to a white and gets brighter as the time grows closer to the alarm. For the 30 minuites after the alarm has sounded the light will remain a bright red, signaling that it is time to wake up for the day. In small batch testing this has proven to be an effective method of reducing stress during sleep.
